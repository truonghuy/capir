Detailed Description

Base class of all NPCs.

NPCs could move and do special actions such as shoot or figh. Special actions do not affect the visibility of monster in a maze w.r.t the agents. Monster always belongs to one specific maze.

Author:
Huy Nguyen
Date:
Dec 2010

double Monster::OptimalProb

Probability of which this NPC executes its intended action. When OptimalProb is less than 1.0, there's a small chance this NPC acts randomly.
